ESCALATED 2026-07-19. The single recurring taste failure across large hand-written Descent card batches is **redundancy** — the batch regressing to a handful of templates — not per-card weakness. Individual copy is usually strong; the deck-level feel goes same-y in play.

Confirmed in THREE decks now:
- POISON PITCH (cards 036–119): E4/E5 mirror-template fatigue + premise-dup (early/late, borrowed-and-kept, funeral-laugh, "who'd miss you least"). See [[poison-systemic-taste-debt]].
- RED FLAG RALLY (cards 053–112): a 60-card batch collapsed into ~7 repeated templates. The offenders:
  - "ranks/scores/tiers your people" (friends or party guests): 5 cards (068/071/079/080/101)
  - "keeps a written ledger/binder/dossier of your grievances or flaws": 070/090/102/110/112 (+ existing 012)
  - "backup/second partner on standby / control group": 077 ≈ 086, plus 097
  - "rehearses your breakup/eulogy in the mirror": 073 ≈ 105 (near-identical), 089
  - "you must pass their family/friends' vetting/vote/quiz": 081/093/111/112 (+087/100)
  - "the ex is still around (key/number/gifts/dinner)": 091/099/103/109
  - "pilot / relationship has a scheduled expiry": 074 ≈ 107
  - plus three separate "list on the fridge" cards (068/090/106) and ~6 professions reused twice (defense attorney, sommelier, jeweler, locksmith, pilot, nurse).
- ALIBI DROP (accusations 029–097): same shape. The E4/E5 "secret document that catalogs/ranks your friends" cluster (spreadsheet/list/folder/collage: 064/081/083/090/073), plus internal near-dup pairs (second wallet 060≈086, drawer of labeled keys 067≈097, hidden shrine 057≈085, speech-hijack 061/078/096) and phrase-reuse vs existing ("house none of us has ever seen" 088 = existing 17). Secondary: E3 register-monoculture (funeral+food+gross) and a death motif in ~25% of cards. See [[alibi-systemic-taste-debt]].

**The single most entrenched template now appears in ALL THREE decks: "you keep a secret ranking / ledger / dossier of your friends."** POISON, RED FLAG RALLY, and ALIBI each independently over-produced it. Treat it as a banned default — if a new card is "protagonist secretly ranks/files/scores the group," it needs a fresh premise unless it's clearly the best 1 of its cluster.

Second, secondary pattern specific to RED FLAG RALLY: a **tone-drift toward clinical coercive-control** (Intake logs, marked water glasses, marble "chances," timing your crying, red-penning your journal). Individually within house tone; in AGGREGATE the batch skews darker/more-surveillance than the existing 52 (which were playful-macabre: freezer, go-bag, teeth jars, wetsuit). Density risks reading as an abuse PSA rather than dive-bar-2am. Watch the ratio.

**Why:** the taste law (`HELLDECK/CLAUDE.md`) bans statistical-average output; redundancy is generic-by-repetition even when each card is well-written. This is now the dominant, entrenched failure mode of these batches.

**How to apply:** For any future Descent deck audit, do a premise-dedup pass FIRST (cluster the batch by template, keep the best 1–2 per template, cut/rewrite the rest onto fresh premises), then score individual merit, then check the tone ratio. Report BOTH an individual-merit score and a set-level score after redundancy is applied — they diverge a lot (RED FLAG RALLY: ~93% individual vs ~75% set-level).
